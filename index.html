About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Naik family, our store began as a small venture to share authentic family recipes with the local community. What started as a modest shop in the heart of Mumbai has now grown into a beloved destination for food enthusiasts seeking genuine Indian flavors.

At Naik's Traditional Foods, we take pride in preserving age-old recipes and cooking techniques passed down through generations. Every product in our store is crafted with care, using premium ingredients sourced from trusted farmers across India.

Our Values

  • Authenticity in every flavor
  • Quality ingredients with no compromises
  • Sustainable packaging and practices
  • Supporting local farmers and communities
